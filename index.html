About Me

I am Mamadou D'Elhadj Bah, currently a second-year student at Rochester Institute of Technology majoring in Web and Mobile Computing with a minor in Management Information Systems. I was born in Maryland and grew up in Guinea, West Africa. Besides English, I am fluent in French and my native language, Pular.

I've traveled to several countries, including France, Belgium, Netherlands, Germany, Canada, and Senegal. My journey with technology started at the age of 5, when my father bought a computer for us to use. This sparked my passion for gaming and technology.

My greatest strength is my ability to learn quickly and adapt to new concepts with ease. However, I sometimes struggle with procrastination when I am confident in my understanding of a task.I have recognized that this approach is not always effective, and I am actively working on improving my time management and productiv.

I aim to become a more independent person and make new friends with similar interests. I am hard-working, responsible, ambitious, enthusiastic, and friendly. I enjoy helping people and contribute positively to any community I am part of.
